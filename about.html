ABOUT

Mirko Borsche was born in December of 1971 in Tegernsee, Germany. He was a graffiti artist in his youth before moving to London to study at Kingston University from 1992-1993, where he earned a post-graduate degree in Graphic Design.

"Graphic design is not art. We give form to brands, with the clients of our clients in mind."

He then returned to Germany and earned a diploma in Communication Design at the University of Applied Sciences Augsburg. He has an extensive background in corporate advertising and progressive cultural design. And in 2007, he founded his Munich-based design firm, Bureau Borsche.
